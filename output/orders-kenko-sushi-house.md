## Manager feedback (last 5 weeks)

The recent catering sessions have yielded a mixed but generally positive performance, with several dishes consistently receiving favourable feedback. Several key items have demonstrated particular strength, notably the Teriyaki Salmon rice bowl, which was repeatedly praised for its quality and flavour, particularly on May 11th and June 1st. The Chicken Karaage ricebowl has also been a consistent success, consistently noted for its crispiness, as seen on May 11th, May 18th, and June 1st. The Japanese Chicken Katsu, as highlighted on May 4th, was consistently well-received, and the Chicken Parmi, chips and salad remains a reliable and popular choice, particularly on May 18th when it was described as perfectly cooked and generously portioned. The Lamb wrap has also been a strong performer, receiving positive feedback across multiple sessions, with the May 25th session noting it as the best of the term.

However, recurring issues have been identified. The Sweet and Sour Chicken has been a point of concern, with the sauce frequently described as over-sauced (May 4th) or thin (June 1st), indicating a need for greater consistency in sauce preparation. The Creamy Udon broth has also presented a recurring weakness, with the broth being noted as thicker than usual on May 4th and thin again on May 18th and June 1st, suggesting a variation in recipe execution. Beef Fried Rice and Mongolian Beef and Rice have generally been well-received, with positive comments regarding tenderness and flavour on May 11th, May 25th, and June 1st. The marinated tofu element within the Teriyaki Salmon rice bowl was specifically highlighted as a successful element on May 11th. Kenko continues to be a strong offering within the price range, as consistently noted. Further investigation into the consistency of the Sweet and Sour Chicken sauce and the Creamy Udon broth preparation is recommended.
