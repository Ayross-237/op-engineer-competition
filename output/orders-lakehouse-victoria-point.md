## Manager feedback (last 5 weeks)

The following summary details catering feedback received between 5th May and 2nd June 2026. Overall, the caterer has demonstrated consistent quality in several dishes, however, there are areas requiring attention to maintain a high standard.

Several dishes were consistently praised throughout the period. The Korean Beef Bulgogi was frequently highlighted as a standout, with multiple sessions noting its tenderness and excellent marinade, particularly on 12th and 19th May and 26th May. The Japanese Chicken Curry also received positive feedback, described as reliable and well-executed, with the vegetarian version specifically commended for its aubergine swap on 5th May. The Fried Chicken Burger with chips was consistently rated as ‘great’, with particular attention paid to the chip’s crispness on 5th May and 2nd June. The Sweet and Sour Chicken was also considered solid and well-balanced on 19th May.

However, recurring issues were identified. The Classic Cream Pasta consistently received negative feedback, with the sauce frequently separating and being described as unremarkable on 5th May, 19th May and 2nd June. The Shrimp Fried Rice experienced problems with heat retention, becoming lukewarm on 12th May. Furthermore, the Fish Taco Bowl was a significant concern, with broken taco shells and overcooked, dry fish noted on 19th May and 2nd June. The Gnocchi in Tomato Sauce was occasionally problematic, with instances of sticky gnocchi reported on 12th May and 26th May, but perfect execution was noted on 26th May. Spaghetti Bolognese was a standout on 19th May, praised for its richness and portion size. 

Moving forward, attention should be directed towards improving heat retention for the Shrimp Fried Rice and addressing the issues with the Fish Taco Bowl, specifically regarding fish preparation and taco shell integrity. A review of the Classic Cream Pasta recipe is recommended to ensure consistent sauce quality.
