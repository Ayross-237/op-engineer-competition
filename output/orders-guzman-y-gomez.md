## Manager feedback (last 5 weeks)

The catering service has demonstrated significant consistency in several key dishes throughout the review period, receiving overwhelmingly positive feedback across multiple sessions. Notably, the Chicken Enchilada has been consistently praised as a standout, achieving ‘perfect’ status in four of the five sessions, with particular commendation for the cheese-pull quality on the 2026-06-01 delivery. The Pulled Pork Burrito Bowl is another consistently successful offering, receiving ‘outstanding’ or ‘great’ ratings in every session reviewed. The Caesar Salad also maintains a high level of satisfaction, with both versions consistently described as ‘perfect’ or ‘crisp and well-dressed’. 

The Crispy Chicken Taco remains a reliable favourite, consistently receiving positive feedback for its crunchiness. Several dishes, including the Chicken Quesadilla and Nacho Fries, are frequently highlighted as standouts, with the Nacho Fries particularly praised for their crispness and well-topped presentation, achieving ‘standout’ status on multiple occasions. The Breakfast Tacos have shown improvement, with the 2026-05-18 session marking a successful execution of the dish, specifically noting the soft eggs and warm tortillas. 

However, there are areas requiring attention. A recurring issue has emerged regarding the Nachos, with the 2026-05-25 session highlighting a return to soggy consistency, attributed to packaging inconsistencies. This needs to be addressed to ensure consistent quality. While the Cali Burrito is generally considered ‘good’ or ‘solid’, it doesn’t consistently receive the highest praise. The Grilled Chicken Burrito is also consistently rated as ‘solid’, suggesting an opportunity to elevate this dish to meet the standards of other successful offerings. Further investigation into the preparation and packaging of the Nachos is recommended to resolve the ongoing quality concerns.
