## Manager feedback (last 5 weeks)

The recent catering sessions have yielded a mixed but generally positive feedback profile. Several dishes consistently performed well, demonstrating a strong understanding of customer preferences. Mie Goreng, across both versions, was a standout, frequently described as excellent and reliable. Beef Pad Thai received particularly positive comments, with multiple instances noting the moistness and balanced flavour of the beef, alongside tender cuts. The Lemongrass Grilled Beef and Noodles, both with and without vegetables, were consistently praised as the best versions offered to date, with the marinated mushrooms receiving specific positive feedback regarding flavour retention. 

Chinese Honey Soy Noodles remained a dependable, mid-range option, consistently receiving positive reviews for its reliability. The Bacon Carbonara has been a popular choice, with the caterer achieving a consistently rich and creamy execution, particularly in the most recent session. Stir-fry Noodles topped with Chicken and Spaghetti Meatballs also received positive feedback, though with some variation in quality. 

However, there were areas for improvement. The Spicy Miso Udon experienced inconsistency, with one session noting a thin broth lacking depth, while another described it as at peak. The Creamy Garlic Beef Noodles proved problematic, with several instances reporting an over-rich sauce that students struggled to finish, and a subsequent session highlighting a heat retention issue resulting in lukewarm temperatures. Furthermore, the Bacon Carbonara suffered from the sauce breaking again in one session, indicating a potential issue with preparation or storage. Finally, the Lemongrass Grilled Beef and Noodles vegetarian version was noted as slightly under-seasoned in one session. Overall, the caterer’s strengths lie in dishes like Mie Goreng, Beef Pad Thai, and the Lemongrass Grilled Beef and Noodles, while addressing the issues with the Spicy Miso Udon broth consistency and the Bacon Carbonara sauce breakage would be beneficial.
